The Wonder

My Favorite Film One Of My Favorite Films Is Wonder Directed By Stephen Chbosky.

The Movie Tells The Story Of A Young Boy Named August "Auggie" Pullman, Who Was Born With A Facial Difference.

Stars Jacob Tremblay As Auggie, Julia Roberts As His Mother, And Owen Wilson As His Father.

Film Is About How Auggie Starts Attending A Regular School After Years Of Homeschooling.

He Faces Many Challenges Because People Treat Him Differently Due To His Appearance.

One Of The Most Important Moments In The Film Is When Auggie Wins The School's “ Henry Ward Beecher Medal,”

Showing That He Is Not Only Brave But Also Kind. This Moment Shows That People Should Be Judged By Their Character, Not Their Looks.

The Main Idea Of Wonder Is That Kindness Can Make A Big Difference In The World, And We Should Always Be Compassionate To Others.

In Conclusion, Wonder Teaches Us An Important Lesson About Acceptance And Kindness, Making It A Film Everyone Should Watch.
